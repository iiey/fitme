Private, just for you

Why host it yourself

  • Your data is yours. It never leaves your computer unless you decide to connect something.
  • No account, no fees. FitMe is free and open-source - nothing to subscribe to.
  • It keeps working. No service to shut down, no API limits, no surprises.

Smart with your history

  • Knows your export. Drop in Garmin or Strava - FitMe figures out which is which.
  • No double-counting. The same workout from two apps is recognised and counted once.
  • Every sport, done right. Each activity shows only the stats that actually apply to it.
Getting your data in

Bring years of history in minutes

Download your export from Garmin or Strava, drop it into FitMe, and you're done. Want it kept up to date automatically? Connect Intervals.icu and new workouts arrive on their own.

Drop in your export

  • Drag in your Garmin or Strava export and FitMe sorts out the rest - no setup, no command line.
  • Re-import anytime. An updated export simply adds what's new and never creates duplicates.
  • Your routes, heart rate, power and more are read straight from the files you already have.
  • Big imports run in the background, so you can start exploring right away.

Or keep it in sync

  • Connect once with Intervals.icu and new workouts show up on their own.
  • It only fetches what's new, so staying current is quick and quiet.
  • Activities you've already imported are recognised - never counted twice.
  • Runs automatically in the background, about once a day.
Made to feel instant

Fast, even after years of training

A long history shouldn't mean a slow app. FitMe is careful about what it loads and when, so pages open quickly and charts and maps stay smooth - whether you have one season or ten.

Opens quickly
FitMe only works out the numbers a page actually shows, so even a deep archive loads fast.
Loads as you scroll
Sections appear right when you reach them, so the page is ready the moment it opens.
Smooth maps
Routes are simplified just enough to keep their true shape, so panning and zooming stay fluid.
Light on space
Detailed workout data is stored compactly, keeping your whole history small on disk.
